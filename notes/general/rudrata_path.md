>[!tldr] Rudrata path
>Given a [graph](graph.md) ([undirected graph](graph.md) or [directed graph](directed_graph.md)) $G = (V,E)$  a [path](path_(graph).md) $p$ is a *Rudrata path* if it visits every vertex exactly once.
