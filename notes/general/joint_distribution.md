>[!tldr] Joint distribution
>Given two [random variables](random_variable.md) $X$ and $Y$ over domains $A$ and $B$. The *joint distribution* of $X \oplus Y$ is over $A \times B$ and is given by
>$$\mathbb{P}[X \oplus Y = (a,b)] = \mathbb{P}[X=a, Y=b].$$
