>[!tldr] NP-hard
>A problem $f$ is *NP-hard* if for all [search problems](search_problems.md) $g$ there is a [many-one reduction](many-one_reduction_(problem).md) from $g$ to $f$. In other terms, $f$ is as hard as any [search problem](search_problems.md).
