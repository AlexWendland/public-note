>[!tldr] Packets
>During the process of [encapsulation](encapsulation.md) a packet is a [segment](segment.md) with the additional headers for the destination and source [Internet Protocol (IPv4)](internet_protocol_(ipv4).md). This is sometimes refereed to as the data-gram.
