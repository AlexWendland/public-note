>[!tldr] Restriction bias
>*Restriction bias* occurs from choosing a [modelling paradigm](modelling_paradigm.md), $M \subset Func(A,B)$, as your candidate can not be some functions in the space $Func(A,B)$ you are biased towards the ones in $M$.
