>[!tldr] Broadcast
> You broadcast a message on a [network](network.md) by setting the destination [MAC address](mac_address.md) to be "all f's" this relates to the MAC address with all 1's. This is understood by the [router](router.md) to be sent on to all ports it is connected to.
