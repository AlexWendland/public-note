>[!tldr] Many-one reduction
> There is a *many-one reduction* of problem $A$ to $B$ ($A \rightarrow B$ or $A \leq B$) if a [polynomial time](polynomial_time.md) algorithm to solve $B$ would also solve $A$ in [polynomial time](polynomial_time.md).
