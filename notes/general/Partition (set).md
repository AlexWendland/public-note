>[!tldr] Partition
>Given a set $S$ a partition is a series of subsets $S_i \subset S$ such that they are mutually disjoint
>$$S_i \cap S_j \not = \emptyset \Rightarrow i = j$$
>and make up the whole of $S$
>$$\bigcup_i S_i = S.$$
