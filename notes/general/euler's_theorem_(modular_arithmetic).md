# Statement

>[!important] Euler's theorem
>For integers $n,a \in \mathbb{Z}$ such that gcd(n,a) = 1. Then
>$$a^{\phi(n)} = 1 \ (mod \ n)$$
>where $\phi(n)$ is [Euler's totient function](euler's_totient_function.md).
