> [!tldr] Strongly connected
> Let $G = (V, E)$ be a [directed graph](directed_graph.md). We say two vertices $x,y \in V$ are strongly connected if there exists [paths](path_(graph).md) $x$ to $y$ and $y$ to $x$.
