>[!tldr] Exact prefix hijacking
>This is a form of [BGP Hijacking](bgp_hijacking.md) where the attacking [AS](autonomous_system_(as).md) announces a path for the same prefix as a genuine [AS](autonomous_system_(as).md). This disrupts the flow of traffic to the intended [AS](autonomous_system_(as).md).
