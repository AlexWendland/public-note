# Statement

> [!lemma] Lemma
> In an $n$-player pure strategy game, if elimination of [Strictly dominated strategy](strictly_dominated_strategy.md) eliminates all but one combination of strategies, that combination is the unique [Nash equilibrium](nash_equilibrium.md).
> Similarly a [Nash equilibrium](nash_equilibrium.md) can never be [strictly dominated](strictly_dominated_strategy.md).
