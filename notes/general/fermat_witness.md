>[!tldr] Fermat Witness
>For a number $r \in \mathbb{N}$ a *Fermat witness* is a value $0 < z < r-1$ such that
>$$z^{r-1} \not = 1 \ (mod \ r).$$
>Such a Fermat witness is called trivial if $gcd(z,r) > 1$ and non-trivial otherwise.
