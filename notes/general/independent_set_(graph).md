>[!tldr] Independent set (graph)
>In a [graph](graph.md) $G = (V,E)$ a subset $I \subset V$ is an *independent set* if the [induced subgraph](induced_subgraph.md) on $I$ has no edges. (i.e. for all $(u,v) \in E$ either $u \not \in I$ or $v \not \in I$.)
