>[!tldr] Host (networks)
> A host can have multiple meanings in the context of networks. Most broadly it means any actor that can send and receive traffic on a network.
> In the context of [Internet Protocol (IPv6)](internet_protocol_(ipv6).md) it is a [Node (IPv6)](node_(ipv6).md) that is not a [Router (IPv6)](router_(ipv6).md).
