>[!tldr] Model-based reinforcement learning
>Model-based [reinforcement learning](reinforcement_learning.md) is a method of doing [reinforcement learning](reinforcement_learning.md) where you use the provided transitions to build your model of the problem then use a planner such as [Value iteration (MDP)](value_iteration_(mdp).md) or [Policy Iteration (MDP)](policy_iteration_(mdp).md) to produce a policy.
