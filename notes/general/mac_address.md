>[!tldr] MAC address
>A MAC address is a 48-[bit](bit.md) address that represent physical devices. These are made human readable by representing them as 12 hex digits (these are grouped into 2 characters and separated by - for windows : for Linux and in groups of 4 separated by a . SISCO routers and switches)
