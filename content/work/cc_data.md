## Overview

In my roles as Data Scientist and later as Head of Data at [CC Data](https://ccdata.io/) (formerly [CryptoCompare](https://www.cryptocompare.com/)), a cryptocurrency data provider, I spearheaded product development and data quality management during a pivotal transition towards the institutional market.

## Key Achievements

**Product Development**
- Worked closely with clients to customize data sets to their specific needs
- Introduced comprehensive metrics for data quality monitoring
- Developed a product for integrating Decentralized Exchanges (DEXs) from on-chain data

**Team Leadership & Process Improvement**
- Established a problem-centric work approach to improve the data team's effectiveness
- Transitioned the data tech stack to Kubernetes
- Implemented a CI/CD pipeline using GitHub Actions

**Cost Optimization**
- Conducted proactive review of Azure service usage
- Achieved significant cost reduction through optimization initiatives

**Business Impact**
- Products developed under my tenure contributed substantially to the company's annual revenue
