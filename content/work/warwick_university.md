## Overview

I was employed by the [Warwick Maths department](https://warwick.ac.uk/fac/sci/maths/) and [Computer Science department](https://warwick.ac.uk/fac/sci/dcs/) as a teacher and lecturer during my PhD studies.

## Teaching Experience

**Lecturing**
- Delivered lectures for the 4th year course Group Theory
- Covered lectures for the 3rd year course Groups and Representations

**Support Classes**
- Ran support classes for multiple modules across Mathematics and Computer Science
- Modules included: Analysis 1, Discrete Maths and its Applications 2, Experimental Maths, Geometric Group Theory, Group Theory, Groups and Representations, Lie Algebras, and Set Theory

**Teaching Range**
- Delivered teaching across all formats, from 1-1 sessions with struggling students to running 400-person lectures
