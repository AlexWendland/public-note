>[!definition] Mixed strategy
>In [Game theory](game_theory.md) a *pure strategy* is non-deterministic i.e. you have access to a random variable $X$ that can be used to make your strategy $s: S \times [0,1] \rightarrow A$ from states of the game and the result of the random variable to actions.
