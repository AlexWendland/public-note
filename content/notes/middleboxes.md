>[!definition] Middleboxes
>These are devices on a network that effect traffic but do not server the role to propagating messages within a network. Such examples are [firewalls](firewall.md), [NAT boxes](network_address_translation_(nat).md), [Load balancer](load_balancer.md), and [Intrusion detection systems](intrusion_detection_systems_(ids).md).
