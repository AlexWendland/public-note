>[!definition] Internet Protocol Stack (IPS) 4 layers
>The IPS stack is a model of how communication should work over the internet. It is structured into 4 different layers:
>1. Application layer
>2. Transport layer
>3. Internet layer
>4. Network access layer
>
>It is based on the [OSI model](open_systems_interconnection_(osi)_model.md) and compares as follows.
