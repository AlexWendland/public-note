>[!tldr] Router (IPv6)
>Is any [Node (IPv6)](node_(ipv6).md) that forwards [Internet Protocol (IPv6)](internet_protocol_(ipv6).md) [Packets](packets.md) not addressed to itself. This is the definition from [RFC2460](rfc2460_internet_protocol,_version_6_(ipv6)_specification.md).
