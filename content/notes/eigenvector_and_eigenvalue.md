>[!tldr] Eigenvector and Eigenvalue
>Given a field $F$ and [matrix](matrix.md) $A \in M_{n,n}(F)$ an *eigenvector* is a vector $v \in M_{n,1}(F)$ such that
>$$ A v = \lambda v$$
>for an associated *eigenvalue* $\lambda \in F$.
