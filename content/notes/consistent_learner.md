>[!tldr] Consistent learner
>Suppose we are in the [modelling framework](modelling_framework.md) with some training data $T$ and a [hypothesis space](modelling_paradigm.md) $H$. A *consistent learner* $h \in H$ is one where $h(a) = b$ for all $(a,b) \in T$.
