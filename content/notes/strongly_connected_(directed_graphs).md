> [!definition] Strongly connected
> Let $G = (V, E)$ be a [directed graph](directed_graph.md). We say that two vertices $x, y \in V$ are strongly connected if there exist [paths](path_(graph).md) from $x$ to $y$ and from $y$ to $x$.
