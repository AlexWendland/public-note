title: Connected (graph)
type: definition
---

A [graph](graph.md) $G = (V, E)$ is considered connected if for all vertices $x,y \in V$ there exists a [path](path_(graph).md) $\{v_i\}_{i=1}^k$ such that $v_1 = x$ and $v_k = y$.
