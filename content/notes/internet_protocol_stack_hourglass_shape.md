title: Internet protocol stack hourglass shape
type: explainer

When you look at the number of [protocols](protocol_(networks).md) at each layer of the [OSI model](open_systems_interconnection_(osi)_model.md) you notice that there is an hour glass shape. With [layer 7](layer_7_application.md) and [layer 1](layer_1_physical.md) having a variety of [protocols](protocol_(networks).md) in them buts [layer 3](layer_3_network.md) only using a single [protocol](protocol_(networks).md) the [Internet Protocol (IP)](internet_protocol_(ip).md). [layer 4](layer_4_transport.md) is dominated by [UDP](user_datagram_protocol_(udp).md) and [TCP](transmission_control_protocol_(tcp).md).
