>[!tldr] Uniform distribution
>For a domain $D$ of size $\vert D \vert = N$ then the *uniform distribution* on $D$ is the [probability distribution](probability_distribution.md) given by
>$$p(d) = \frac{1}{N} \mbox{, for all } d \in D.$$
