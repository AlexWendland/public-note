>[!definition] Fold (cross validation)
>Given some [training data](training_data.md) $T$ for [cross validation](cross_validation.md) we [partition](partition_(set).md) $T$ into $T_i$ for $1 \leq i \leq k$. A *fold* of $T$ is one of these partitions.
