# Statement

> [!lemma] Lemma
> For an [Ergodic Markov chain](ergodic_markov_chain.md) with [unique](ergodic_markov_chains_have_a_unique_stationary_distribution.md) [stationary distribution](stationary_distribution_(markov_chains).md) $\pi$ we have
> $$\lim_{k \rightarrow \infty} P^k =  \left ( \begin{array}{ccc}
> \cdots & \pi & \cdots\\
> \cdots & \pi & \cdots\\
> \vdots & \vdots & \vdots\\
> \cdots & \pi & \cdots\\
> \end{array}\right ).$$
