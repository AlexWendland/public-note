> [!definition] Intradomain routing
> *Intradomain routing* is the process of routers within an [AS](autonomous_system_(as).md) discovering their routing table. The main class of [protocols](protocol_(networks).md) used here are [Interior gateway protocol (IGP)](interior_gateway_protocol_(igp).md).
