> [!definition] Distributed Denial-of-Service (DDoS)
> This is an attack against a service where you flood it with requests that it cannot handle. This causes the service to crash, denying access to it for other users.
> ![DDoS Diagram](../../static/images/DDoS_diagram.png)
> The diagram demonstrates the simplest form of attack, but this has many variants such as [Spoofing](spoofing.md) and [DDoS reflection and amplification](ddos_reflection_and_amplification.md).
