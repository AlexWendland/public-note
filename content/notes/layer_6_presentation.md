>[!definition] Layer 6 Presentation
>Layer 6 of the [OSI model](open_systems_interconnection_(osi)_model.md) is responsible for telling us the form the data is in. How should we break down the long binary expression into something the application can understand.
>
>For example in [HTTP](hypertext_transfer_protocol_(http).md) it says characters are grouped into 8 [bit](bit.md) sequences and interpreted as extended [ASCII](american_standard_code_for_information_interchange_(ascii).md) characters.
