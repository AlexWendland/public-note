>[!definition] Rudrata cycle
>Given a [graph](graph.md) ([undirected graph](graph.md) or [directed graph](directed_graph.md)) $G = (V,E)$  a [cycle](cycle_(graph).md) $c$ is a *Rudrata cycle* if it visits every vertex exactly once.
