>[!definition] Conditional probability
>For two events $A$ and $B$ the *conditional probability* of $A$ happening given $B$ has happened is
>$$\mathbb{P}[A \vert B] = \frac{\mathbb{P}[A \cap B]}{\mathbb{P}[B]}.$$
