> [!definition] Reverse directed graph
> Suppose we have a [directed graph](directed_graph.md) $G = (V,E)$. Define the *reverse directed graph* as $G^R = (V, E^R)$ where
> $$E^R = \{(w,v) \mid (v,w) \in E\}.$$
