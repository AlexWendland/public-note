> [!definition] Connected components
> In a [graph](graph.md) a connected components is a maximal [connected](connected_(graph).md) [subgraph](subgraph.md) $G'$ of $G$.

A graph $G$ can only have multiple connected components if it is not [connected](connected_(graph).md).
