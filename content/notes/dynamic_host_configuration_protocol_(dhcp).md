>[!definition] Dynamic Host Configuration Protocol (DHCP)
>To connect to the internet you need 4 bits of information:
>- Your [Internet Protocol (IPv4)](internet_protocol_(ipv4).md),
>- Your [network mask](network_mask.md),
>- The [default gateway](default_gateway.md) of your local network, and
>- The [Domain Name System (DNS)](domain_name_system_(dns).md) server you will use.
>
>The DHCP will provide you all the above information once you connect to a new network. When you first join a network you send DHCP discover message and will be provided with all your information.
