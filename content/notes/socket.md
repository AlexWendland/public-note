>[!definition] Socket
>A socket is the collection of 3 pieces of information:
>- A transport protocol such as [TCP](transmission_control_protocol_(tcp).md) or [UDP](user_datagram_protocol_(udp).md),
>- An [Internet Protocol (IPv4)](internet_protocol_(ipv4).md), and
>- A [port](port.md).
>
>They are used by applications to send and receive data. This is the PO box for this application on the internet.
