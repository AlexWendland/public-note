> [!definition] Hyperplane
> A hyperplane in an $n$-dimensional [vector space](vector_space.md) $V$ over a [field](field.md) $\mathbb{F}$ is an $n-1$ dimensional subplane. This is given by $n-1$ [linearly independent](linearly_independent.md) vectors $v_1, v_2, \ldots, v_{n-1} \in V$ and a point $x \in V$. Then the space is made of
> $$
> P = \{x + \sum_{i=1}^{n-1} \alpha_i v_i \mid \alpha_i \in \mathbb{F}\}
> $$
> Note: different vectors $v_1, \ldots, v_{n-1}, x \in V$ could define the same hyperplane.
