>[!tldr] Complement graph
>Given an [undirected graph](graph.md) or [directed graph](directed_graph.md) $G = (V,E)$. We define the *complement graph*
>$$G^C = (V, \{(u,v) \in V \times V \ \vert \ (u,v) \not \in E\}).$$
