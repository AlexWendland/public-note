# Statement

> [!lemma] Lemma
> In the [Simulated Annealing](simulated_annealing.md) algorithm with some unspecified assumptions, we have
> $$\mathbb{P}[\text{ending at } a] = \frac{e^{f(a)/T}}{Z_T}.$$
