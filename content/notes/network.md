>[!definition] Network
> A network is just a grouping of [hosts](host_(networks).md) that have the same network requirements. Though more specifically they are [hosts](host_(networks).md) that are directly connected by switches. The [hosts](host_(networks).md) will have to same 24-[bit](bit.md) [subnet](subnets.md).
