>[!tldr] Router (IPv6)
>Is any [[Node (IPv6)]] that forwards [[IPv6]] [[Packets]] not addressed to itself. This is the definition from [[RFC2460 Internet Protocol, Version 6 (IPv6) Specification|RFC2460]].
