>[!tldr] Fold (cross validation)
>Given some [[Training data|training data]] $T$ for [[Cross validation|cross validation]] we [[Partition (set)|partition]] $T$ into $T_i$ for $1 \leq i \leq k$. A *fold* of $T$ is one of these partitions.
