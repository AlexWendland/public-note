>[!tldr] Rudrata cycle
>Given a [[Graph|graph]] ([[Graph|undirected graph]] or [[Directed graph|directed graph]]) $G = (V,E)$  a [[Cycle (graph)|cycle]] $c$ is a *Rudrata cycle* if it visits every vertex exactly once.
