>[!tldr] Complement graph
>Given an [[Graph|undirected graph]] or [[Directed graph|directed graph]] $G = (V,E)$. We define the *complement graph* 
>$$G^C = (V, \{(u,v) \in V \times V \ \vert \ (u,v) \not \in E\}).$$
