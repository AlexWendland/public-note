>[!tldr] Optimisation problem
>For an *optimisation problem* we are given an input space $A$ and a fitness function $f: A \rightarrow B$ where $B$ is ordered (usually $\mathbb{R}$). We want to find
>$$\mbox{arg}\max_{a \in A} f(a).$$
