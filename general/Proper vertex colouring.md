>[!tldr] Proper vertex colouring
>Given a [[Graph|undirected graph]] $G = (V,E)$ and [[Vertex Colouring|vertex colouring]] $c : V \rightarrow D$. This colouring is called *proper* if for all $(u,v) \in E$ we have $c(u) \not = c(v)$.
