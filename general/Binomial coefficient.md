>[!tldr] Binomial coefficient
>Let $0 \leq k \leq n$ for $k,n \in \mathbb{n}$ then define the *binomial coefficient* of $n$ choose $k$ as
>$$\binom{n}{k} = \frac{n!}{k!(n-k)!}.$$
