>[!tldr] Rudrata path
>Given a [[Graph|graph]] ([[Graph|undirected graph]] or [[Directed graph|directed graph]]) $G = (V,E)$  a [[Path (graph)|path]] $p$ is a *Rudrata path* if it visits every vertex exactly once.
