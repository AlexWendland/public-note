>[!tldr] Mutual information
>Suppose we have two [[Random variable|random variables]] $X$ and $Y$ over different [[Function domain|domains]] $A$ and $B$. Then the *mutual information* is defined to be
> $$I(X, Y) = H(Y) - H(X \vert Y).$$
