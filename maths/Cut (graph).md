>[!tldr] Cut
>Let $G = (V,E)$ be a [[Graph|undirected graph]]. A *cut* of $G$ is a *partition* of $V = S \cup \overline{S}$. The *cut edges* are
>$$cut(S,\overline{S}) = \{(v,w) \in E \vert v \in S, w \in \overline{S}\}.$$
>
