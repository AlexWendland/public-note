> [!tldr] Reverse directed graph
> Suppose we have a [[directed graph]] $G = (V,E)$. Define the *reverse directed graph* to $G^R = (V, E^R)$ where
> $$E^R = \{(w,v) \ \vert \ (v,w) \in E\}.$$
